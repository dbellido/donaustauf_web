Activities

Photogrammetry

Photogrammetry is the science of creating 3D information from photographs. Nowadays, just a commercial camera is required to acquire the images. From terrestrial or aerial plattformas photogrammetry is one of the most suitable techniques in the digiization of Cultural Heritage.

Read more

Laser scanner

The purpose of a 3D laser scanner is to create a point cloud of the surface of a objetc. Nowadays there a wide amount of scanners in the market and they can acquire information in small and large scenes. The three principles of laser scanners include Time of flight (TOF), Phase Shift and Triangulation-based systems.

Read more

Structured light scanner

A structured light scanner is a 3D intruments to achieve the 3D shape of a object. It uses projection light patterns and a camera system (usually two cameras). Projection band of lights onto an object, produces line distorted from the different perspectives, and it is used to extract the 3D information about the object.
